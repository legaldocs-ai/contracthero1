What happens after you subscribe?

After your payment is confirmed, you will be redirected to your Personal Desk page. That page is your private workspace for this plan. You can submit tasks there at any time during your current billing period.

Please bookmark your Desk page in your browser. If a billing period ends and your subscription is not renewed, your Desk page will stop accepting new tasks and will gently redirect you back to the public Pricing page.

How Desk fees work (simple version):

You see one clear price (for example, $280). Contributors and tools are paid from that amount. The Desk fee is the spread, which covers scoping, tone management, and quality control.
