What happens after you subscribe?

The Desk stays small on purpose. The goal is not to flood you with content, but to help you say important things properly, at the right time, in a tone that fits your relationships.

01 · Intake
We build a quiet profile

We note who you write to, your role, your risk comfort and tone preferences. For 30+ clients, this often includes family, a small group of investors, or a handful of long-term clients.

02 · Flow
You send tasks in simple language

You do not have to speak “AI”. You describe what is happening and what you hope to achieve; the Desk turns this into drafts you can review, adjust and send.

03 · Boundaries
We keep limits and expectations clear

Number of tasks, typical turnaround times and topics we will not touch are all agreed upfront, and revisited if your situation changes.

Questions people ask before starting

Why USD 19.9 for a one-time task?
It is enough to give each task careful attention, while still being an easy “yes” for many US/EU clients 30+. It also keeps the Desk model simple: no coupons, no complexity — just a clear starting point.
Can I move from one-time tasks into a subscription Desk?
Yes. Many clients begin with a single letter or content pack, then move into Starter or Professional Desks when they realise they want this form of support every month.
Can I downgrade or pause a Desk?
Desks are intentionally small and flexible. You can discuss pausing, downgrading or returning to one-time tasks if your workload or finances change.
Do you guarantee financial or legal outcomes?
No. The Desk helps you communicate clearly and calmly; it does not provide legal, tax or investment advice, and does not promise any specific result from your decisions.
Is this only for clients in the US and Europe?
The Desk is optimised for Western-style communication and for English-speaking clients 30+, but you can be based anywhere as long as you are comfortable paying in USD and using standard online payment methods.
