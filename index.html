Frequently asked questions
Clear answers to the most common questions business owners ask before they move away from generic templates and into AI-assisted documents.
Straightforward & transparent
Are you a law firm or giving legal advice?
−
No. ContractHero AI is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We help you generate business documents using AI and standardized structures. You remain responsible for reviewing each document and, when needed, discussing it with your own attorney.
Which countries do you support?
+
Right now we focus on English-language contracts commonly used in the United States, United Kingdom and European Union. Many documents are written in a way that can be adapted in other English-speaking countries, but you should always verify with a local professional if you are unsure.
Can I edit the contracts after generating them?
+
Yes. Every contract can be exported to Word and PDF, or simply copy-pasted into your own format. You are free to edit, add or remove clauses as needed before signing.
Is my data secure and private?
+
We only use your data to generate documents for you. Party details and contract drafts are stored securely and are never sold to third parties. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
Can I cancel my subscription?
+
Yes, you can cancel anytime with a couple of clicks from inside your account. When you cancel, you keep every document you have already generated.
