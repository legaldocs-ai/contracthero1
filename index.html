How AIFreelance Hub works
Simple model: clients post AI-friendly tasks, freelancers apply, the platform earns only when value is created.
1 · Define the task
  • Client describes what they need in plain language.
  • Chooses category: writing, images, data, automation.
  • Sets budget and preferred deadline.
2 · Match with AI freelancers
  • Pre-screened freelancers apply with a short proposal.
  • Client picks the best fit based on scope and price.
  • Work is delivered in 24–72 hours for most tasks.
3 · Approve & share the fee
  • Client approves final work and completes payment.
  • Freelancer receives their share (e.g. 70–80%).
  • AIFreelance Hub keeps a transparent platform fee.
FAQ
A short overview of the most common questions. You can expand this later into a full help center or knowledge base.
Do I need to be a developer?

No. Most AI freelancers here are writers, designers, marketers and operators who are simply good at using AI tools to get things done faster.

Which countries can use AIFreelance Hub?

Clients are mainly from the US, UK and EU. Freelancers can apply from most countries as long as they can receive payment through supported methods.

How does the platform make money?

The platform earns via transparent fees on completed projects, plus optional upgrades for heavy users. No hidden charges, no pay-to-bid spam.
