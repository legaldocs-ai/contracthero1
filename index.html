How the marketplace works
Easy to explain in one message to any client or freelancer: three clear steps.
1 · Client submits a project
  • Short form with budget, task type and deadline.
  • Client can attach links or references if needed.
  • You can help refine the brief if something is unclear.
2 · Match with AI freelancers
  • Shortlist of suitable freelancers is prepared.
  • Client picks the best fit based on scope and rate.
  • Work is delivered in 2–5 days for most projects.
3 · Approve, pay, repeat
  • Client approves work and pays the agreed amount.
  • Freelancer is paid; platform keeps a clear fee.
  • Happy clients come back for ongoing projects.
FAQ
You can keep the answers short now and expand them into a full Help Center later.
Do I need to be a developer?

No. Many successful freelancers here are writers, designers, marketers or operators who simply know how to use AI tools to work faster and smarter.

Which countries can use the platform?

Clients mainly come from the US, UK and EU. Freelancers can apply from most countries as long as they can communicate in English and receive payments.

How does AIFreelance Hub make money?

The platform earns from a transparent fee on completed projects, plus optional “Pro” upgrades later. No hidden charges and no selling user data.
