Before you spam the appeal button

What this tool is — and what it is not

It is a structured way to think like a platform. It is not a magic “unban” button or a law firm.

Is this a magic “unban” service?
No. Some accounts can be recovered, some cannot. The risk snapshot tells you which bucket you are in: reasonable chance, low chance, or almost zero chance. It is better to know than to send ten emotional appeals.
Do you log into my accounts?
No. You stay in full control. You paste notices and facts, you submit appeals from your own devices and networks. The engine never asks for passwords, codes or screenshots.
What AI do you use?
The engine runs entirely on Cloudflare Workers AI. There is no need for an OpenAI key on your side. Your text goes to a Worker you control, and the AI only uses it to generate these results.
Is this legal advice?
No. Aurum Dispute Desk is not a law firm and does not replace your lawyer. It helps you communicate with platforms more clearly and realistically.
