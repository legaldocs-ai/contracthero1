How it works

Three simple flows: clients publish tasks, freelancers deliver, Stripe collects payments.

1. Clients post tasks

Clients describe requirements, budget and deadline. Tasks appear in the Task Hall and can be private or public.

2. Freelancers accept & deliver

Registered freelancers log in, accept tasks, communicate via WhatsApp or email, and upload final work.

3. Stripe collects payments

When clients are satisfied, they pay securely via Stripe Payment Link or Checkout. Your platform fee is included.
