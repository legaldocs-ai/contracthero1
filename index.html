How Aurum Task Desk Runs

Think of it as a small private office between clients and freelancers. You coordinate the relationship; AI and humans handle the work.

Step 01

Clients send tasks in plain language

A US client writes: “I need a letter to my daughter,” or “I need 10 posts explaining my business calmly.” The Desk turns this into a clear scope and budget.

Step 02

Freelancers execute with AI + structure

Contributors use AI tools to draft, but the Desk enforces tone, clarity, and structure so clients feel understood — not spammed.

Step 03

Stripe settlement & desk fee

Clients pay one Stripe amount. Freelancers receive a net payout. The desk fee is the spread — just like a quiet coordination office.

Common Questions

Is this a company or just a tool? It is structured like a small private office. You decide if you present it as a firm, a personal desk, or part of your existing business.
Do clients need to know about AI? No. Many clients just see that their letters, posts, or scripts are done clearly and on time. The Desk quietly uses AI where it helps.
How does the Desk earn money? Clients pay one amount (for example, USD 480). Freelancers or contributors receive a net amount. The difference is the Desk fee, which compensates you for scoping, coordination, and quality control.
