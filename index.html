How AIWorkHub works
Simple for both sides: clients post AI-friendly work, freelancers deliver using AI tools, the platform takes a transparent fee only when value is created.
1
Clients post AI-ready tasks

Businesses describe what they need: “rewrite 10 pages”, “generate 30 product images”, “clean this spreadsheet”, “summarise customer feedback”, etc.

2
AI freelancers apply & deliver

Pre-screened AI freelancers pick tasks they can handle using tools like GPT-4, Midjourney, Claude, automation scripts and more.

3
You take a platform fee

When the task is successfully completed and confirmed, the platform collects a fee (e.g. 20–35%) and releases the rest to the freelancer.
