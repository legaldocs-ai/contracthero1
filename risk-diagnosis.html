What we actually send back

You receive a short written diagnosis in clear language, normally within 24–72 hours. It includes:
  • Risk level (realistic / borderline / very low chance)
  • Key reasons your account might be flagged
  • Whether an appeal is worth it or likely to fail
  • What evidence or documents you should prepare
This free diagnosis is not legal advice and does not guarantee reinstatement. It helps you decide whether to:
  • Write your own appeal with a clearer structure
  • Book a paid escalation letter or full appeal
  • Walk away from a very low-odds fight
We do not: create fake invoices, fake IDs, fake proof-of-address, or help you rent / buy accounts against platform rules.
What to expect from a risk diagnosis

This is a calm, structured look at your case. We treat both small advertisers and large accounts the same way: clear questions, realistic odds, no drama.

1 · We read like a policy team

Your answers are read with the same paranoia a platform risk or policy person would have. We look for the weak points they are likely to see.

2 · We map your realistic options

Sometimes the safest move is to re-build; sometimes a carefully structured appeal is worth it. We explain the trade-offs in normal language.

3 · You decide next steps

If you want help with a paid full appeal or escalation letter, you can book it later. This diagnosis comes first and stays free.
