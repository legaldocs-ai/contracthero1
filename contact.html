How to use this contact form
1. If you already submitted a risk diagnosis, mention it here (same email).
2. If you want a quote, tell us which platform and how critical the account is.
3. Do not paste full passwords, 2FA codes or secret keys here.
Direct channels

Contact form vs. risk diagnosis

Two doors to the same desk — one for general questions and quotes, one for real cases that already triggered a platform notice.

When should I use the contact form?
Use this page when you:
  • • Have questions about pricing or what’s included
  • • Want to discuss a potential VIP case before sending details
  • • Are an agency or business with multiple accounts / platforms
  • • Prefer to have a human look at your situation before you paste any notices
When is the risk diagnosis form better?
Use the Risk Diagnosis page when:
  • • You already have a suspension / restriction / hold notice
  • • You can paste the text or screenshot content of the notice
  • • You want a structured first opinion before spending money
Most real cases should start there. You can always move into Appeal Pack or VIP Guidance afterwards.
