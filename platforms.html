Where this desk can actually help.

We focus on platforms where a clear written story, policy awareness and realistic evidence change how a human reviewer sees your case. Not every “ban” is worth fighting. Below are the places where a structured appeal desk like this usually makes sense.

If your platform is not listed but the pattern is similar (risk systems, compliance, creator accounts), you can still reach out. We will tell you honestly if it is outside our scope.

Supported platforms

Different rules, same idea: the platform is trying to manage risk. Our job is to translate your story into something a risk team can reasonably accept.

High volume · Fast changes
TikTok & TikTok Shop
Creators · Shops · Agencies
Common issues we see:
  • • Live / content bans and community guideline strikes
  • • TikTok Shop product or logistics violations
  • • Payment holds, KYC document problems
  • • “High risk behaviour” around ads or traffic
We help by structuring your history, business model and future controls so it doesn’t look like a random reshipping or arbitrage account.
Start with a risk diagnosis and mention TikTok in the platform field.
Ad accounts & BM
Facebook / Instagram Ads
Media buyers · Brands
Common issues:
  • • Disabled ad accounts and Business Managers
  • • Restricted ability to advertise or create assets
  • • Policy flags on landing pages or creative
  • • Previous history of “circumventing systems”
We cannot unlock every BM, but we can stop you from burning your last appeal with an emotional wall of text and no structure.
For serious ad spend or agencies, ask about VIP Guidance.
Holds & limitations
PayPal
E-commerce · Service providers
Common issues:
  • • 180-day rolling reserve / limitation notices
  • • “Unusual activity” and compliance checks
  • • Documentation reviews for higher volumes
  • • Chargeback and dispute patterns
We focus on explaining your business model, customer journey and risk controls in a way that fits their internal compliance mindset.
High stakes? Mention that your balance or cashflow is critical in your diagnosis.
Risk engines & disputes
Stripe
SaaS · Subscriptions · DTC
Common issues:
  • • Sudden account terminations
  • • Payout holds and rolling reserves
  • • High dispute ratios or refund cycles
  • • Risk on specific verticals or business models
We help articulate why your model is controllable and what changes you’re willing to make to keep risk within their comfort zone.
Stripe cases often justify an Appeal Pack or VIP Guidance.
Trust & safety
Airbnb
Hosts · Co-hosts
Common issues:
  • • Suspended host accounts
  • • Listing removals after guest complaints
  • • Identity and safety concerns
  • • Disputes around reviews or “party risk”
Here, tone and acknowledgement of guest and neighbour experience matter. We help you write like a responsible host, not a defensive argument.
Use the risk diagnosis page and paste the full trust & safety emails.
POA & compliance
Amazon
Private label · Resellers
Common issues:
  • • Account suspensions triggered by product or review issues
  • • Listing removals for IP or safety complaints
  • • Requests for Plan of Action (POA)
  • • Multiple related account flags
We help you create a structured POA with root cause, corrective actions and long-term prevention that doesn’t read like copy-paste from the internet.
Complex Amazon cases should be discussed via the contact form.
Other & mixed cases
Other platforms
Creators · Agencies · Tools
We sometimes take cases on:
  • • Google Ads, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit Ads
  • • Marketplaces, booking platforms, creator tools
  • • Affiliate networks and payment processors
The pattern is the same: risk systems and human reviewers. If your situation involves fake documents, account rental or obvious abuse, we will decline the case.
Unsure if your platform fits? Send a short note via the contact page.
