Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Aurum Task Desk (“we”, “us”, “the Desk”) provides private task coordination, writing services, and AI-assisted drafting for clients and contributors. We take privacy seriously, especially because many clients share sensitive personal stories, business information, and family communication details.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number.
  • Task content: letters, instructions, notes, or materials provided by clients.
  • Account details: login information you provide for accessing your Desk.
  • Technical data: IP address, device info, analytics (anonymous).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for:

  • Completing writing and coordination tasks.
  • Communicating with you about your task or account.
  • Quality control and tone adjustments for older clients.
  • Processing payments (via Stripe or other processors).

3. Confidentiality & Data Handling

Task content is treated as confidential working material. We do not resell, publish, or share client content with external parties. You may request deletion of drafts or personal information after a task is completed, except where legal or accounting rules require limited retention.

4. AI Usage

The Desk uses AI tools for drafting, structure, and improving clarity. No raw AI output is sent directly to clients without human review.

5. Payment Processing

All payments are processed via secure third-party providers (e.g., Stripe). The Desk does not store full credit-card details.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Basic analytics may be used to improve the Desk’s performance. Cookies may be used for login sessions and language preferences.

7. Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to your stored information
  • Correction of inaccuracies
  • Deletion of content or account (where legally possible)

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries or data requests, please contact us via the Contact page.
