Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Aurum Task Desk (“we”, “our”, “the Desk”) provides AI-assisted writing and task automation services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect: email address, task descriptions and files you voluntarily submit, billing information (handled securely by our payment processors), and usage data such as page visits and basic analytics.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information only to operate and improve the Desk, including:

  • To provide writing and task services you request
  • To communicate with you about your tasks or account
  • To process payments through trusted third-party providers
  • To comply with legal obligations where required

3. Payment Processing

All payments are securely processed by our third-party partners (for example: Paddle, Wise or others chosen by the customer). We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

4. Data Retention

Task content is stored only for as long as needed to complete your order and maintain service quality. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct or delete your personal information, and to object to certain types of processing. We will make reasonable efforts to honor such requests.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please email us at support@aurumdesks.com (or the address listed on our website).

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the Desk or applicable laws. We will update the “Last updated” date when changes are made.
