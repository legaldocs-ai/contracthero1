How AIFreelance Hub works for clients
A lightweight flow built for people who are busy. You stay focused on approvals and feedback, not on mastering prompts or managing full-time hires.
Step-by-step overview
1 · Define your task
  • Describe what you need in simple language: text, images, data or workflows.
  • Add any examples, brand guidelines or references if you have them.
  • Pick a budget range and rough deadline.
.
2 · Receive proposals from AI freelancers
  • Pre-screened freelancers review your task and send short proposals.
  • You see their starting price, timeline and how they plan to use AI.
  • You pick the freelancer who best fits your style and budget.
3 · Review and request changes
  • Get drafts, previews or partial results to review quickly.
  • Ask for changes or clarifications – AI makes iterations faster.
  • Approve when you’re satisfied with the output.
4 · Approve and complete payment
  • Once everything is approved, you complete payment for the project.
  • The freelancer gets paid and AIFreelance Hub retains a platform fee.
  • You can re-order from the same freelancer or post a new task.
